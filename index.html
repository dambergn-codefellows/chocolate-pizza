Chocolate Pizza

POSTED ON 15 DEC 2013 / DESERTS

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over the medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to disolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stiring frequently, until you hav a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice Cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely disolved and the mil is just barely lukewarm. whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using and ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturerer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup masccarpone
1/2 tsp pink salt
1lb Black Mission Figs
1/2 cup brown sugar
1-4 tbsp water
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1/3 granulated sugar
2 egg yolks
1 lemon, juiced
2 tbsp butter
1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
